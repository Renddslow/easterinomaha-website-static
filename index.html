Listen, we know how scary it can be to walk into a new church for the first time. That's why we want to make you feel as comfortable as possible. You won't be met with guilt, shame, or condemnation. And we don't care how you're dressed or what candidate you voted for.

What to Expect

If it's your first time joining us for Easter you're in for a real treat. Our Easter experience is designed to help you and your family connect to the story of the Resurrection in a brand new way.

In total, a Flatland Church service will last no more than 60 minutes.

The service will begin with a creative presentation that is sure to capture your imagination.

Our lead pastor Bart Wilkins will deliver an inspiring, hope-filled message about the Resurrection of Jesus.

Following the message the Flatland worship band will lead the congregation in worship, song lyrics will be projected onto the screen so you can sing-along and/or engage with worship however you feel comfortable.


What About My Kids?

Every Sunday our amazing team of staff and volunteers create a vibrant and exciting environment for kids to experience the love of God and move closer to Him, and Easter Sunday is no exception. We will have activities for your kids from birth to 5th grade.

About Flatland Church

Life can be hard, especially when you're balancing work, family, and what little free time you have. Not to mention trying to grow closer to God and chasing the good life. That's why at Flatland Church our one passion is to help you move closer to the center of God's kingdom where real life transformation happens.

We guarantee that moving to the center of God's kingdom will have a dramatic, positive impact on every area of your life, from character to home, relationship to industry, society to talents.
